# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve Awesome Claw.

## What belongs here?

Good entries are useful to OpenClaw users or builders, such as:

- OpenClaw skills
- plugins and integrations
- coding-agent / ACP tooling
- deployment templates
- real workflows and examples
- community projects
- high-quality learning resources

## Quality guidelines

Before submitting, check that the project:

- is directly relevant to OpenClaw or its ecosystem
- has a working link
- has a clear README or documentation
- is useful to someone other than the author
- is not spammy, misleading, or primarily promotional

## Entry format

Use this format:

```markdown
- [Project Name](https://example.com) - Short description ending with a period.
```

Guidelines:

- Keep descriptions concise.
- Start descriptions with a capital letter.
- End descriptions with a period.
- Do not use tracking links.
- Avoid marketing language.
- Put entries in the most specific category.
- Prefer alphabetical order within each category once sections grow.

## Pull requests

Please include:

- why the entry belongs here
- which category it should be listed under
- any relevant context about maintenance/status

Maintainers may decline entries that do not fit the quality bar.

## Paid or featured listings

Awesome Claw does not currently sell placement.

If paid sponsorships or featured listings are introduced later, they must be clearly labeled. Undisclosed paid placement is not allowed.

## Reasons an entry may be declined or removed

Maintainers may decline or remove entries that are:

- not directly relevant to OpenClaw
- low quality or undocumented
- abandoned or unsafe
- misleading, spammy, or overly promotional
- duplicates of a better canonical source
- primarily affiliate/SEO content

## Project updates

For project updates, follow [@awesomeclawhub](https://x.com/awesomeclawhub).
